Activity type descriptions

    Commission: the balance of commission fees paid and rebated
    Corporate actions: the balance of cash earned and paid through non-exchange events (e.g., option exercises and assignments, stock splits, mergers, acquisitions, rights issues, spin-offs, etc.)
    Deposits: the value of all cash deposited to your account
    Dividends: the value of all dividends earned
    Dividend reinvestment: the value of dividends re-invested to buy more securities
    Fees and rebates: the balance of fees and rebates
    FX conversion: the net amount of cash converted between CAD and USD
    Interest: the balance of interest earned and paid
    Others: the balance of fees paid and rebated not categorized elsewhere
    Transfers: the value of assets transferred in and out of your account
    Withdrawals: the total amount withdrawn from your account
